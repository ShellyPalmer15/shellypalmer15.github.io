Get the Most Out of your CES® Experience

VIP Floor Tours

ShellyPalmer and the International CES are pleased to offer attendees informative show floor tours during the 2015 CES. Whether you’re a first-time attendee or a seasoned CES veteran, choose from options ranging from a General Overview tour to customized tours, all designed to enhance your CES experience. With the introduction of the all-new CES Tech East and Tech West this year, our tours will help you navigate your way to what you want to see most.

Get to the future first Take an official CES show floor tour, presented by ShellyPalmer™ and the International CES. The guided CES show floor tour is the ultimate experience. Our friendly and knowledgeable guides will escort you on a two-hour walking tour of the show floor.

  • CES Tech East Tours (LVCC - North Hall, Central Hall and South Hall) will showcase innovative technologies from today’s top categories.
  • CES Tech West Tours (The Sands Expo) will feature companies and innovations shaping the future.

We’ll help you understand the story of consumer technology and how it impacts how you live and work in a connected world.

Tour Features:

  • Small groups led by expert tour guides
  • The ShellyPalmer 2015 CE Trend Report
  • Wireless headsets so you won't miss a word
